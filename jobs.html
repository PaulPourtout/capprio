ABOUT US

Heetch is a ride-sharing app with a simple mission: help you enjoy your night out.

The service is open every night and our drivers are doing everything to make your ride, and your night, unforgettable and friendly: good music, candies and beverages in every car!

The platform is growing very fast and we face a lot of challenges with thousands of rides every night in Paris, Lyon, Lille, Warsaw, Stockholm and soon many more cities in Europe.

We have a lot of exciting things to do and we are looking for the right people to grow with us!
