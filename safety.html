Safety

Heetch's mission to make the night more accessible, which means in particular of to make it safer That's why safety is an essential and integrated at all levels of our service, our passengers' additional insurance, our Passenger's Well-Being team available 7/7, but also the functionality of the application (mutual notations, etc. .).

The heetch insurance

Our insurance to € 2m, first of its kind in Europe, covering passengers and Heetch drivers, as they have booked a particular driver via our platform:
- Additional Coverage for bodily damage suffered by passengers in the amount of maximum € 500,000 per person, or € 2 million per car.
- This complementary insurance is in added to personal insurance of our drivers (checked at registration).
Download the insurance certificate

Monitoring of drivers

Each driver is the subjected to a meticulous control at registration and must provide the following documents:
- Clean criminal record
- Driving license older than 6 months
- Insurance and car registration

Ratings and Comments

Users have the power! Passengers and drivers self-assess after each ride. If the average of a user is too low, his account will be automatically suspended. Every morning we read, process and read very seriously every comments.

Zero tolerance

Heetch has zero tolerance for alcohol and drugs. In case of suspicion, please call us at 06 44 63 98 90.
